How to play / How does it works?

The yellow elements are coins. You have to collect 20 of it so as to win the game.

After collection of the 20 coins, you pass through the exit page, then you've completed the task. Otherwise, if you pass through the exit, you'll be directed back to the begining page.

Also, the green elements are obstacles. If you hit them, Game Over
